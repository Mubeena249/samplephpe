BUSINESS SOLUTIONS >ADVERTISING

1 in 3 Indians are on PhonePe. We understand how users spend and manage their money. This helps us enable our partners to target and engage with India’s digitally transacting user-base.

How Does it Work?

We offer two in-app properties that you can use to drive awareness and growth
for your products/services, Brand Ads and Rewards.


If your business goal is to enhance awareness, brand ads can help increase visibility and drive user engagement. We display these ads in the form of banners on the app homepage, Offers page, Rewards page etc, based on the product/service relevance. These banners are high-impact properties that guarantee impressions from our digitally savvy users. We can also help you target these banners to relevant user cohorts.


Using PhonePe Rewards, we distribute personalized offers and coupons to our transacting customers. You can run an offer or share your existing offers with relevant users in the form of these rewards. These are always present in the rewards section to ensure visibility, and we also nudge our customers post-delivery to use them. Rewards, as a product, is designed to drive conversions. It is geared for impact with deep targeting algorithms, high impressions per coupon and product features designed to drive growth. Once a user clicks on it, they can know all about your business through ‘About the Merchant’ & ‘Steps to Avail Offer’ sections. The ‘Gift Now’ button allows them to gift the coupon to other users in case they don’t need it.
